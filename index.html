Warm-Up 1 / 20

Root Stomp & Bounce

00:58

Stand barefoot. Bounce gently from both knees — heels barely leave the ground. Arms hang completely loose. After 20s add a soft heel-stomp on each downbeat.

Mula Bandha — light
How to perform
  1. Stand barefoot on the floor.
  2. Bounce gently from both knees — heels barely leave the ground.
  3. Let arms hang completely loose and shake naturally.
  4. After 20 seconds, add a soft heel-stomp on each downbeat.
  5. Keep the whole body soft — jaw relaxed, shoulders dropped.
